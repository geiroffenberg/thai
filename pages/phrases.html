How To Speak Phrases

Use these common phrase patterns and swap in your own words.

Introducing Yourself

ฉันชื่อ... (chan cheu...) - My name is...

Asking Price

อันนี้เท่าไหร่ (an nee tao rai?) - How much is this?

Ordering Food

ขอ...หนึ่งที่ (kho... neung tee) - I would like one...
