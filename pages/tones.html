Thai Tone Trainer

Tones are meaning. Same consonants and vowels can become different words when your voice moves.

The 5 Thai Tones

  • Mid (เสียงสามัญ): steady, level voice
  • Low (เสียงเอก): starts mid and drops lower
  • Falling (เสียงโท): starts high and falls
  • High (เสียงตรี): starts higher and stays high
  • Rising (เสียงจัตวา): starts low and rises up

Important: tone marks help, but final tone also depends on consonant class and whether the syllable is open or closed.
